About us

We started our journey in a cheese factory located on a mountain, about 20 minutes away from our village, San Sebastián de La Gomera, known among the inhabitants of La Gomera as "La Villa". I spent my childhood with my grandad, may him god rest in peace, and he taugh me everything about the goats. He taugh me how to milk a goat, what do they eat, how to split them on the land, how to identify syntomns of sickness, basically everything I did need to know to start with these business.
